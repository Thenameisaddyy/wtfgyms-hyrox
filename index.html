What is HYROX?
The world's fastest growing
indoor fitness race.

HYROX is a global fitness race held indoors — same format in every city worldwide. Run 1km, complete a workout challenge, repeat 8 times. That's 8km of running and 8 physical challenges in one event. Open to everyone.

8
🏃
Run + Challenge × 8
Every round is 1km of running followed by one physical challenge. Eight rounds total — 8km running + 8 challenges.
🌍
🌍
Same format worldwide
Delhi, London, New York — always the same 8 challenges in the same order. Train at WTF, race anywhere in the world.
∞

Open to everyone
Beginner or elite, 20 or 55. No qualification needed. HYROX has a category for every fitness level and race format.
